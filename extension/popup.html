iPath

Please follow the steps below :

  • Open devtools panel.
    • Right-click anywhere on the webpage.
    • Click Inspect.
  • In the right side of Elements tab, click on iPath tab.
  • Here you will get the XPath and you can also edit and evaluate it.
  • To use the CSS selectors features, change the dropdown value from XPath to CSS. It will give the unique CSS selector and you can also edit it and verify any CSS.
